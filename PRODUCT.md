# Product

## Register

product

## Users

RetailOps has two visible audiences. The public landing page speaks to viewers who need an immediate premium impression: project evaluators, technical reviewers, and anyone judging whether the system feels professional. The authenticated product is for store operators: admins, cashiers, and warehouse staff who need fast, accurate workflows for POS, stock, orders, suppliers, debt, reports, users, and permissions.

## Product Purpose

RetailOps brings sales, inventory, purchasing, debt, reports, authentication, and role-based workspaces into one microservices-backed retail operations system. The landing page should make the project feel cinematic, polished, and memorable before sending users into the application. The app UI should remain clear, durable, and efficient for real daily operations.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, precise, confident. The landing page should feel like a 4K product trailer for a serious operations platform: dark, high-contrast, dimensional, and alive with background motion. The product workspace should feel focused, controlled, and easy to scan.

## Anti-references

Do not preserve the old white-and-blue landing look. Avoid default Vuetify marketing layouts, generic SaaS card grids, plain static hero sections, tiny decorative motion only on cards, and any landing page that feels like a template. Avoid making the internal app look like a flashy trailer surface; the strong cinematic treatment belongs on the landing page.

## Design Principles

1. Make the first viewport feel premium immediately.
2. Use motion as atmosphere and system storytelling, not decoration alone.
3. Separate the brand showcase from the operational workspace.
4. Keep retail workflows readable, fast, and role-aware.
5. Preserve accessibility and performance even when the landing page is visually ambitious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The interface should target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Strong landing-page motion needs a clear `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback that keeps the page beautiful without animated movement. The app surfaces should avoid distracting background motion during POS, inventory, and admin work.
